About

The 2021 8th Annual Louisiana Conference on Computational Biology and Bioinformatics is being held {{page.eventDate}} {{page.eventLocation}}. The conference is co-sponsored by the Louisiana Biomedical Research Network (LBRN) and the LSU Center for Computation and Technology (CCT). The conference aims to expose Louisiana to the cutting edge of Computational Biology, Bioinformatics Research and Applications while also providing a platform for exchange of information and technical knowledge among Louisiana-based scientists involved in different aspects of computational biology & bioinformatics.


Tentative Conference Schedule (all times are CDT):

  • April 15th, Thursday from 12:30pm to 5:30pm CDT
  • April 16th, Friday from 12:30pm to 5:30pm CDT
  • April 17th, Saturday from 9:00am to 12:00pm CDT
